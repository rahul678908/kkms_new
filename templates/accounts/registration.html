Registration Instructions

Please follow these steps to complete your KKMS registration:

  • Download and print the registration form
  • Fill out all required fields with accurate information
  • Attach required documents and photographs
  • Submit the completed form to our office or authorized representatives
  • Keep a copy for your records

Required Documents

Please ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Driver's License)
  • Address proof (Utility bill, Bank statement, etc.)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Educational certificates
